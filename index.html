AI for Science

I am passionate about using machine learning to learn more about how the world works.

I am an incoming PhD student in Computer Science at NYU's Courant Institute of Mathematical Sciences.

The following two questions drive many of my current research endeavors.
1. How can we build models that effectively capture the underlying dynamics of complex systems (like the brain)?
2. How can these insights be used to enhance our models?
My current interests include representation learning, inverse problems, generative AI, imaging, neuroscience, reinforcement learning, and cognition.

In my free time I enjoy listening to music and playing the guitar, bass clarinet, and piano.
